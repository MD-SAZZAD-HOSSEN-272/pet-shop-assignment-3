Frequently Asked Any Question

Do you sell pet food and accessories?

Yes! We have a wide range of pet food, toys, beds, cages, grooming supplies, and more.

Can I adopt a pet from your shop?

Absolutely! We collaborate with local shelters to help pets find loving homes.

Are the pets vaccinated?

Yes, all our pets receive necessary vaccinations before they are available for purchase or adoption.

What are your shop's opening hours?

Our shop is open from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you sell exotic pets?

We primarily sell common pets, but we sometimes have exotic pets like reptiles and birds. You can check with us for availability.

Can I return a pet if I change my mind?

We have a return policy under specific conditions. You need to return the pet within 7 days with a valid reason, and it must be in good health.
